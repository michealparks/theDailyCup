About | Calendar | Location | Menu | Contact | Blog

We're a locally owned coffee shop.

We import our coffee from South America and take advantage of a variety of brewing methods, from conventional paper filtration to flash steaming the perfect espresso. We feature a diverse selection of hot or iced coffees that reflect the ever expanding world of coffee preparation.

By the way, our baristas are extremely talented.

We prepare our food in a homecooked style, using cuban presses for our sandwiches and oven baking for our pies. We have a large array of breakfast, lunch, and snack items ready to complement any sort of mood you're in.

Oh, and our quiches are unrivaled.

Whether you're looking for a place to play a game of chess, have a meeting with a few colleagues or coworkers, or just want a good view of Charleston daily life, we think we'll be able to accommodate you.

Check out our calendar for upcoming events.

Our location has recently changed, and we'll be sure to start updating you about our exciting new downtown location soon.
